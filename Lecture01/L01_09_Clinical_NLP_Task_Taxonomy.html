Clinical NLP Task Taxonomy
Named Entity Recognition (NER)
Identify diseases, medications, symptoms, procedures
Ex: "Patient has diabetes and takes metformin"
Relation Extraction
Find relationships between medical entities
Ex: "Drug X causes Side Effect Y"
Temporal Expression
Normalize time expressions and event sequences
Ex: "2 weeks ago" → specific date
Negation Detection
Identify negated medical concepts
Ex: "No signs of infection" vs "Infection present"
Medical Code Mapping
Map clinical text to ICD-10, CPT, SNOMED codes
Ex: "Hypertension" → ICD-10: I10
Clinical Text Summarization
Generate concise summaries from clinical notes
Ex: Discharge summary from hospital records
