Bayesian Reasoning Integration
Bayesian Framework
Prior Probability: Pre-test likelihood based on prevalence
Likelihood Ratio: How much test changes probability
Posterior Probability: Post-test diagnostic certainty
Sequential Testing: Chain multiple tests together
Bayes' Theorem in Diagnosis
P(Disease|Test) = [P(Test|Disease) × P(Disease)] / P(Test)
Critical: Integrating prevalence and test characteristics improves diagnostic accuracy
