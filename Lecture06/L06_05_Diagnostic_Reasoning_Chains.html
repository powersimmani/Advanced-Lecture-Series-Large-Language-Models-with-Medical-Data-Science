Diagnostic Reasoning Chains
Example: Chest Pain Evaluation
Symptoms:
65yo male, sudden chest pain, radiating to left arm, diaphoresis
Differential:
1) MI, 2) Angina, 3) GERD, 4) Costochondritis, 5) Pulmonary embolism
Key Tests:
ECG (ST elevation), Troponin (elevated), Cardiac enzymes
Diagnosis:
Acute ST-elevation myocardial infarction (STEMI)
Reasoning:
Classic presentation + ECG changes + elevated biomarkers → STEMI confirmed
