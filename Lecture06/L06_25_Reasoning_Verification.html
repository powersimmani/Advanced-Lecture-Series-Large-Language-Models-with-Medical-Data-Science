Reasoning Verification & Quality Control
Verification Steps
Logical Consistency: Check for contradictions in reasoning chain
Evidence Support: Verify each claim has supporting data
Completeness: Ensure all relevant factors considered
Guideline Adherence: Confirm alignment with clinical standards
Verification Checklist
✓ Are all steps logically connected?
✓ Is each conclusion supported by evidence?
✓ Were alternative diagnoses considered?
✓ Are contradictory findings explained?
✓ Does final answer match clinical guidelines?
✓ Are safety concerns addressed?
