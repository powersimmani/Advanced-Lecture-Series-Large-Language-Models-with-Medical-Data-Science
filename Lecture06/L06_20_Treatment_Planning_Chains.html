Treatment Planning with CoT
Sequential Decision Process
Treatment Options: List first-line, second-line alternatives
Risk-Benefit Analysis: Weigh efficacy vs adverse effects
Patient Factors: Consider comorbidities, preferences, adherence
Monitoring Plan: Define follow-up and adjustment criteria
Example: Hypertension Management
Options: ACE-I, ARB, CCB, Diuretic, Beta-blocker
Patient: 55yo, DM, no CAD, eGFR 60, K+ 4.2
Reasoning: ACE-I preferred (DM protection) → check for contraindications (none) → assess tolerability risk (low)
Plan: Start Lisinopril 10mg daily
Monitoring: BP recheck 2 weeks, Cr/K+ 1 week, target BP <130/80
Guideline-based + personalized = optimal treatment
