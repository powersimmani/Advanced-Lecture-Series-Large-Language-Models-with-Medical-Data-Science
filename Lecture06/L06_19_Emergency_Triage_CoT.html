Emergency Triage with Chain-of-Thought
Triage CoT Process
Rapid Assessment: ABC (Airway, Breathing, Circulation) first
Priority Scoring: ESI (Emergency Severity Index) 1-5
Resource Prediction: Anticipate needed tests/interventions
Time-Critical Flags: STEMI, stroke, sepsis alerts
Example: Chest Pain Triage
Critical: CoT enables rapid, systematic prioritization under time pressure
