Lab Result Interpretation with CoT
Anemia Workup Interpretation Process
CoT Reasoning Steps
Step 1: Anemia confirmed (Hgb 8.5 < 12 g/dL) → Severity: moderate
Step 2: Microcytic (MCV 68 < 80 fL) → DDx: Fe deficiency, thalassemia, chronic disease
Step 3: Low ferritin (8 ng/mL) + high TIBC → Confirms iron deficiency
Step 4: Etiology? → Female, menorrhagia likely → Consider GI loss if persists
Diagnosis: Iron deficiency anemia → Treat + investigate source
Integration: Labs + clinical picture = complete assessment
