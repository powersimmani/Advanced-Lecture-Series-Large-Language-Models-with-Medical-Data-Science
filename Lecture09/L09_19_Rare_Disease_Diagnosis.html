Rare diseases are very difficult to diagnose due to small patient populations and diverse symptoms. On average, it takes 5-7 years to reach an accurate diagnosis, during which patients cannot receive appropriate treatment.
Recent advances in AI technology have made it possible to address these challenges. Few-Shot Learning enables learning with limited data, and similar case retrieval allows utilization of past diagnostic cases. Additionally, integration with medical knowledge graphs has enabled more accurate diagnoses.
