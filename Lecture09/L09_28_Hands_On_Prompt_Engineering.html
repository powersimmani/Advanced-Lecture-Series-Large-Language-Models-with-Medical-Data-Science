üìù What is Prompt Engineering?
Prompt engineering is the technique of systematically designing and optimizing input instructions to effectively communicate with AI language models. In the medical field, where accuracy and safety are directly linked to patient lives, designing clear, specific, and context-aware prompts is essential.
üè• Applications in Medical Data Science
When applying AI to various medical tasks such as patient diagnosis assistance, medical image analysis, clinical note summarization, and drug interaction checking, proper prompt design significantly enhances model performance. It is also important to write prompts that consider medical ethics, privacy protection, and clinical validity, while clearly defining the role as a tool to assist healthcare professionals' final judgment.
